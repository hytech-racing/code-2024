vnpy.conv — Conversion utilities¶

The vnpy.conv module provides basic conversion utilities.

vnpy.conv.deg2rad(val)¶

Conversion methods between radians and degrees.

vnpy.conv.fahren2celsius(val)¶

vnpy.conv.kelvin2celsius(val)¶

vnpy.conv.kelvin2fahren(val)¶

Conversion methods between Celsius, Fahrenheit and Kelvin.

vnpy.conv.ypr_rads2quat(val)¶
vnpy.conv.ypr_degs2dcm(val)¶
vnpy.conv.ypr_rads2dcm(val)¶
vnpy.conv.quat2ypr_degs(val)¶
vnpy.conv.quat2ypr_rads(val)¶
vnpy.conv.quat2dcm(val)¶
vnpy.conv.dcm2ypr_degs(val)¶
vnpy.conv.dcm2ypr_rads(val)¶

Conversion methods between yaw, pitch, roll in degrees (ypr_degs) and radians (ypr_rads), quaternion (quat), and direction cosine matrix (dcm).

vnpy.conv.velocity_ned_xy2course_over_ground(velocity_x, velocity_y)¶

Converts x,y velocity components in NED frame to course over ground.

vnpy.conv.velocity_ned2course_over_ground(velocity_ned)¶

Converts velocity in NED frame to course over ground.

vnpy.conv.velocity_ned_xy2speed_over_ground(velocity_x, velocity_y)¶

Converts x,y velocity components in NED frame to speed over ground.

vnpy.conv.velocity_ned2speed_over_ground(velocity_ned)¶

Converts velocity in NED frame to speed over ground.

vnpy.conv.quat2omega_phi_kappa_rads(val)¶

Converts an orientation in quaternion representation to omega, phi, kappa in radians.

vnpy.conv.dcm2omega_phi_kappa_rads(val)¶

Converts an orientation in direction cosine matrix representation to omega, phi, kappa in radians.

vnpy.conv.ypr_degs2omega_phi_kappa_rads(val)¶

Converts an orientation in yaw, pitch, roll in degrees to omega, phi, kappa in radians.

vnpy.conv.ypr_rads2omega_phi_kappa_rads(val)¶

Converts an orientation in yaw, pitch, roll in radians to omega, phi, kappa in radians.
